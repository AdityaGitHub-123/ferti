Farmer Guidance & Best Practices

Expert advice to maximize your field's potential.

🌱 Soil Improvement

Consider crop rotation and adding organic matter to improve soil structure and water retention over time.

💧 Water Management

Water deeply but less frequently to encourage deep root growth. Avoid waterlogging which deprives roots of oxygen.

⚙️ Fertilizer Application

Apply fertilizers during the cooler parts of the day. Split doses are generally more effective than a single large application.

🌿 Crop Care

Monitor regularly for pests and diseases. Early detection drastically improves the effectiveness of interventions.
